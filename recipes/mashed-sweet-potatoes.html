Mashed Sweet Potatoes

Description

These easy mashed sweet potatoes are beautiful in their simplicity. Subtly sweet and wonderfully creamy, you'll come back to this mashed sweet potato recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Boil the sweet potato chunks in salted water until tender.
  2. Drain and transfer to a bowl.
  3. Mash with potato masher, then slowly add milk.
  4. Stir in the butter and syrup until melted and well-blended.
